⚠️ Risks, Gaps & Unknowns Your App Is Not Addressing

Even after your model, there are critical blind spots. Here is a structured list with mitigation suggestions.

Known Unknowns (we know we don’t know, but could)

Gap Why It Matters How to Mitigate
Actual farm mortality rate Model assumes 10% pre‑weaning, but real could be 20% → profit halved. Add a simple input: “How many lambs died last year?” Default to 15% if unknown.
Distance to nearest abattoir / market Transport cost model assumes 100km, but real could be 300km → huge cost difference. Use browser geolocation or ask for town name. Integrate with Google Maps Distance Matrix API.
Soil type & carrying capacity Carrying capacity varies 2x between sandy and clay soil. Ask: “Is your soil sandy, loam, or clay?” Use lookup table to adjust ccRate.
Theft history Theft can wipe out 5-30% of flock annually. Insurance recommendation depends on this. Ask: “Have you lost sheep to theft in last 2 years? (Yes/No) If yes, estimate %.”
Labour skill level Skilled herder vs casual worker affects lamb survival. Ask: “Do you have a full‑time herder?” Adjust labour cost and weaning rate.
Wool micron (Merino) 16µ wool fetches R290/kg, 22µ only R70/kg. Ask for micron test result. If unknown, assume average 19µ (R150/kg).

Unknown Unknowns (we don’t know we don’t know)

Risk Description How to Detect / Hedge
Disease outbreak (e.g., Rift Valley fever) Can kill 70% of lambs in a season. Not in model. Add a “disease risk” module: local outbreak alerts from DALRRD, vaccination reminders.
Sudden market collapse China stops buying wool → prices drop 50%. Provide price trend graph with 5‑year volatility. Recommend income diversification.
Climate shift (drought cycles) 2‑year drought reduces carrying capacity by 70%. Integrate with South African Weather Service long‑term forecast. Recommend drought insurance or fodder banks.
Predator surge (jackal, caracal) Can kill 20+ lambs per month. Ask about predator fencing; include jackal‑proof mesh in fencing recommendation.
Policy change (land expropriation, export bans) Unpredictable. Include “political risk” disclaimer and recommend keeping liquid reserves.

Structural Risks in Your App’s Design

Risk Mitigation
User never enters missing data After first run, send SMS/email reminder: “To improve your plan, answer 3 quick questions.” Gamify with “accuracy score”.
User trusts the report blindly Add a “What if?” slider – let them adjust feed cost or lamb price to see profit change. Builds intuition.
User doesn’t act Offer a “weekly action reminder” via WhatsApp. Integrate with Trello or Asana API to create tasks.
Model is too complex to maintain Freeze version 1.0. Only update lookup tables annually via JSON file from your server.
