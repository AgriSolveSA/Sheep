💰 Payment Integration (PayFast / Yoco – both SA)

No recurring fees for the core report – just a one‑time payment. Example using PayFast (no monthly cost, only transaction fee).

Backend webhook: PayFast calls your server on success. Then generate the PDF and email it to the user, also save it in their account.
