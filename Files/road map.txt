🎯 Core Principle: Revenue First, Complexity Later

Your fastest path to cash is the one‑page action sheet for sheep farmers (R499) + ecosystem membership (R99/month). This requires:

· A simple landing page (brochure website)
· A farm input form (with smart defaults)
